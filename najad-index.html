Built for those

A Najad is not a compromise. It is the result of over 50 years of refining a single idea -- that a sailing yacht should be as comfortable and safe offshore as it is a pleasure to live aboard in harbour.

Every boat is built to order in Henan, Orust. The same island, the same hands, the same uncompromising standards that have defined Najad since 1971.

Our Philosophy
"Safety, elegance and perfection -- as well as ease and simplicity in the running of the boat."

We build yachts for people who know what they want and have the means to get it. People who take pride in the quality of everything around them.

A Najad is the sum of countless decisions made without compromise. The joinery. The keel profile. The way the galley works at 30 degrees of heel. None of it is accidental.
